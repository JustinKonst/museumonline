History

Our adventure started in a garagebox in 2008 the hague, with the two friends Hasan Tasdemir and Pascal Rappailles. The garagebox became cramped in no time, so they had relocate to a warehouse in an industrial area. Unfortunatly, the warehouse wasn't properly heated so in winter everything had to be transferred to a vacant building. Eventually this building had to be cleared out aswell. The former collection of 10 arcade videogames were stored in a old showroom for classic cars. But because there was too little space to renovate or play the games, The search for a fitting place continued.

Activities

As soon as you walk into our museum, You'll be greeted by everything that makes gamingculture beautiful and impressive. Follow our timeline and discover the history of videogames through the most important events, games technical advances and artwork. Play the Classics, meet gaming icons, and rediscover the lost videogame treasures. The museum has around 231 consoles, homecomputers and arcade machines waiting for you to be played! Also with 327 unique spots to play, making our museum the biggest arcade in all of europe!
